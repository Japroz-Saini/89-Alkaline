What is Alkaline Water?

Alkaline water is water that has been ionized, which means the pH level of water has been increased. The pH level is a number that measures how acidic or alkaline a substance is on a scale of 0 to 14
If the pH level is 1, it means the water is very acidic and if it is 13, it is very alkaline. Alkaline water which has pH level of about 8 or 9 is said to be neutral. Alkaline water neutralises the acids in the body; thanks to its alkaline nature.
Your body secretes out a lot of acidic juices. and drinking alkaline water will neutralise these acids. Doctors suggest for having foods that are at least 70 percent alkaline and 30 percent acidic.This process of neutralising acids in the body helps prevent various ailments.

Benefits of Alkaline water

Water is important to every cell, tissue, and organ in your body. The best way to stay hydrated: glug-glug-glug glasses of water throughout the day.

Some research has been done on the effects of alkaline intake on bones. A study published in Bone found an effect on bone resorption. Bone resorption is the process where old bone cells are broken down and replaced by new ones.
Less bone resorption and more mineral density result in better bone strength. The authors concluded that “a bicarbonate- and calcium-rich alkali mineral water decreased bone resorption more than a calcium-rich acidic mineral water.”
